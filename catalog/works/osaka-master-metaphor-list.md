A comprehensive catalog of conceptual metaphors compiled at UC Berkeley
and maintained by Osaka University. Contains ~200 metaphors organized
by target domain, drawn from multiple publications in cognitive
linguistics. The primary structured source for the cognitive linguistics
canon import project.
