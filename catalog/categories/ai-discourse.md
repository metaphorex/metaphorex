Metaphors actively shaping how people build, regulate, and understand
artificial intelligence systems. Unlike historical metaphor domains where
the framing has settled, AI discourse metaphors are being contested in
real time -- "tool" versus "agent" versus "oracle" carry different
political and design implications. This category tracks metaphors where
the choice of source domain is itself a consequential act.
