Metaphors rooted in defense, perimeters, and adversarial thinking —
firewalls, attack surfaces, trust zones, shields, locks. Spans infosec,
physical security, national security, and criminology. Structurally
characterized by binary distinctions (inside/outside, trusted/untrusted,
safe/compromised) and the persistent tension between openness and control.
