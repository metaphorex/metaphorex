The mechanical transformation of data through sequential stages — ingestion,
validation, transformation, and output. Borrows heavily from fluid dynamics
(streams, pipelines) and manufacturing (assembly lines). Useful as both a
source and target domain: we process grief, digest information, and filter
noise in everyday language.
