Visual perception: seeing, light, darkness, clarity, focus, and perspective.
The privileged sensory modality in Western epistemology -- knowledge IS sight
in a way it is not smell or taste. We see the point, observe the facts, look
at the evidence. The frame's dominance reveals a cultural bias: not all
languages or traditions equate understanding with vision.
