The trajectory of a human life from birth to death, encompassing
the choices, stages, difficulties, relationships, and purposes
that constitute a biography. As a target domain, life is one of
the most heavily metaphorized concepts in any language -- we
understand it through journeys, stories, games, battles, and
seasons because its full span exceeds any single experience.
