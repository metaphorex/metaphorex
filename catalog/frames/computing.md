The broad domain of mechanical and electronic computation. Parent
frame for more specific computing concepts (programs, data processing,
abstraction layers). As both source and target domain, computing is
unusual — it borrows metaphors heavily (bugs, viruses, memory) while
also exporting them (processing, bandwidth, bandwidth as cognitive
capacity).
