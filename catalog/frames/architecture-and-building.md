The design, construction, and maintenance of physical structures. Encompasses
the full built environment: foundations and framing, facades and interiors,
fire barriers and building codes, ornamental surfaces and load-bearing walls.
The dominant metaphor family for software development and knowledge work —
we build, architect, scaffold, and demolish. Structurally rich because it
separates design from execution, foregrounds the idea that invisible
foundations determine visible stability, and offers the distinction between
public-facing surfaces and hidden internals.
