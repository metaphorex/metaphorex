Tangible things that can be held, given, taken, transferred, and possessed.
One of the most basic source domains in conceptual metaphor theory -- we
understand abstract concepts (ideas, existence, time, emotions) as physical
objects that have weight, substance, and can change hands. The frame provides
the structural backbone for the object case of the Event Structure metaphor
system, where states are possessions, changes are transfers, and causes are
givers of objects.
