Coordinated movement between bodies, ranging from rigidly choreographed
to fully improvised. Structurally rich because it foregrounds partnership,
timing, and the negotiation between structure and spontaneity. Unlike war
(which has winners) or construction (which has blueprints), dance treats
the process itself as the product. The lead/follow dynamic is particularly
productive as a source domain — it encodes mutual responsiveness, not
command and obedience.
