The study of life in the ocean. A surprisingly rich source domain for
metaphors because marine organisms evolved solutions to problems under
extreme constraints (pressure, darkness, salinity) that map well onto
other high-constraint domains. Also home to some of the most bizarre
reproductive and competitive strategies in nature.
