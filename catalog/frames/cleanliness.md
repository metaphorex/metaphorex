The domain of physical cleanliness and contamination: washing, scrubbing,
staining, polluting, purifying, sterilizing. Governs the distinction between
clean and dirty, pure and impure, sanitized and contaminated. The frame
carries strong affective valence — disgust at filth, satisfaction at
cleanliness — which makes it a potent source domain for moral reasoning.
Operates at scales from personal hygiene to environmental pollution.
