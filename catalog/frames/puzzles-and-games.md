The domain of structured challenges with discoverable solutions -- jigsaw
puzzles, riddles, crosswords, and logic games. What makes puzzles distinctive
as a source domain is the promise of completeness: the pieces exist, they fit
together, and the solved state is recognizable. This makes puzzles a
compelling but potentially misleading frame for problems that lack clean
solutions.
