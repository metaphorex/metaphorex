The domain of aiming and shooting at targets: marksman, target, aim,
projectile, hit, miss, precision, scatter. Distinct from war (which
concerns combatants, territory, sides, and victory/defeat) — target
practice isolates the aiming-and-hitting structure without the adversarial
context. The logic is geometric: a fixed point to hit, a projectile
launched toward it, success measured by proximity to center. Source domain
for metaphors about problem-solving precision, goal-directed effort, and
accuracy of analysis.
