The historical practice of communal land use where multiple parties graze
livestock on shared pasture. The original concrete domain behind "tragedy of
the commons" — individual incentives to overuse conflict with collective
interest in sustainability. Structurally rich because it foregrounds
governance, boundaries, and the tension between individual and collective
rationality.
