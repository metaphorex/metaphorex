The deliberate modification of a planet's environment -- atmosphere,
temperature, hydrosphere, biosphere -- to make it suitable for human life.
The frame centers on whole-system transformation: changing not one variable
but an interconnected web of variables simultaneously, guided by a
specification of the desired end state. Key structural features include
the dependency ordering of changes (atmosphere before water before
biology), the multi-generational timescale, and the assumption of
sufficient engineering mastery to reshape an entire world.
