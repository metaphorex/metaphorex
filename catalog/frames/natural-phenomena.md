Geological, volcanic, oceanic, atmospheric, and other natural processes
that operate on scales beyond human control. Lava flows, erosion,
sedimentation, avalanches, floods -- events where material moves, hardens,
accumulates, or erodes according to physical laws rather than human
intention. As a source domain, natural phenomena map uncontrollability and
irreversibility onto human systems: once the lava cools, the landscape is
permanently changed.
