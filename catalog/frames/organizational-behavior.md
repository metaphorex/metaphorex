How groups of people coordinate, compete, and make decisions within
structured institutions. Encompasses org charts, reporting lines,
incentive structures, communication patterns, and institutional culture.
A prolific source domain for software metaphors because organizations and
software systems face structurally similar problems: coordination under
constraints, information flow, delegation, and the gap between formal
structure and actual behavior.
