The public-facing surface of a building, designed to present an appearance
that may differ from the structure behind it. The deliberate gap between
exterior presentation and interior reality makes this frame structurally
rich for metaphors about interfaces, deception, and managed impressions.
The observer's limited vantage point is a key role.
