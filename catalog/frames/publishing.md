The production of documents from templates and variable content.
Publishing separates fixed structure (layout, headings, recurring
sections) from variable content (the specific text, images, data that
fill each edition). This frame maps onto software patterns where a
fixed algorithmic skeleton defines the structure of an operation while
leaving specific steps to be filled in by subclasses or callbacks --
the template is the layout, the variable steps are the content.
