The process and state of bringing disparate parts into a unified whole --
whether in psychic development, organizational alignment, or system
architecture. The central tension is between the integrating center and
the autonomous parts it must encompass without erasing. Productive as a
target domain because nearly every complex system faces the question of
how (and whether) its components cohere.
