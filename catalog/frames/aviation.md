The domain of powered flight, organized around a strict hierarchy of
command and layered systems of redundancy. Aviation is distinctive as a
source domain because it combines extreme consequentiality (errors kill
people) with extreme procedural formality (checklists, standard
phraseology, crew resource management). The pilot-copilot relationship
is not merely one of rank but of complementary attention: the pilot
flies, the copilot monitors, and either can take control if the other
fails. This structure -- shared task, asymmetric authority, mutual
monitoring -- makes aviation unusually productive for mapping onto
human-AI collaboration.
