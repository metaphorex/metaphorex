The patterned interactions among individuals within a social context —
including provocation, status signaling, norm enforcement, and the
management of attention and reputation. Distinct from social-behavior in
its emphasis on dynamic interplay and the production of social effects
(escalation, pile-ons, ostracism, virality) rather than stable structural
roles. Particularly relevant to online and mediated social environments
where anonymity, platform affordances, and audience scale alter the
dynamics of face-to-face interaction.
