The productive friction between complementary or opposing forces that
generates novelty, insight, or adaptation. Not conflict for its own sake
but the structured encounter between different modes -- analytical and
intuitive, structure and spontaneity, insider and outsider -- that
produces outcomes neither side could reach alone. Productive as a target
domain because it captures the generative dynamics of cross-functional
collaboration, creative processes, and dialectical thinking.
