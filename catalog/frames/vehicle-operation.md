The domain of operating vehicles and machinery -- driving, steering,
accelerating, braking, navigating. The driver is the skilled agent who
translates intent into mechanical action through the vehicle's controls.
Productive as a source domain because it encodes a clear agency hierarchy:
the driver knows how to operate the machine; the machine knows how to
interact with the physical world; the passenger need not understand either.
