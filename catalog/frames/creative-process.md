The iterative cycle of generating, shaping, and refining new work — from
initial spark through rough drafts to finished form. Structurally interesting
because it foregrounds the tension between open exploration and disciplined
constraint. The role of the audience as a shaping force, even when absent,
distinguishes this from pure construction.
