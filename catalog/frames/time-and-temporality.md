The experience and conceptualization of time -- duration, sequence, rhythm,
and finitude. Time is almost never discussed in its own terms; it is always
understood through other domains (money, motion, containers, rivers). As a
target domain, time is a magnet for metaphor precisely because direct
experience of it is so elusive.
