What is known but not acknowledged, present but not visible, active but
not accounted for. The structural tension between official knowledge and
operational reality -- the gap between the org chart and how work actually
gets done, between the documented architecture and the runtime system,
between stated values and revealed preferences. Productive as a target
domain because every system of sufficient complexity develops a hidden
layer that its official model does not represent.
