How organisms (including humans) interact in groups. Covers
cooperation, competition, hierarchy, signaling, and norm
enforcement. A rich source domain because social dynamics are
universally experienced and deeply felt — everyone knows what
it's like to be judged, excluded, or elevated by a group.
