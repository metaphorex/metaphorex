The deliberate cultivation of plants — planting, tending, pruning, and
harvesting. Distinguished from wild growth by the presence of a gardener
who shapes outcomes through patient intervention. A rich source domain for
ideas about nurturing, organic growth with guidance, and the tension between
control and natural process.
