The lived experience of having a body — sensation, proprioception,
gravity, fatigue, pain, pleasure. Lakoff and Johnson's foundational
claim: abstract thought is grounded in embodied experience. We
understand UP IS GOOD because standing upright is healthy; we
understand KNOWING IS SEEING because vision is our dominant sense.
One of the most productive source domains in all of metaphor.
