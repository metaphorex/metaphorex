The domain of physical joining -- chains, links, bonds, ties, knots, and
anchors. Connections can be rigid or flexible, direct or mediated, and they
can break. The structural vocabulary maps productively onto abstract
relationships: social ties, hyperlinks, database relations, causal chains.
The frame emphasizes both the connection itself and the possibility of
disconnection -- a broken link, a severed bond, a missing anchor.
