The dynamics of unrealized capability becoming actual -- seeds germinating,
projects launching, ideas taking form. Potential is structurally interesting
because it is defined by what does not yet exist: the gap between current
state and possible future. Emergence adds the insight that what actualizes
is often not predictable from the initial conditions. Productive as a
target domain because it captures the paradox of the new: simultaneously
vulnerable and full of possibility, dependent on conditions yet capable
of transforming them.
