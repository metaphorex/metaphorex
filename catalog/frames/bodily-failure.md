Loss of normal bodily function — from minor malfunction to catastrophic
collapse. Rich in visceral, universally understood imagery. One of the most
productive source domains in informal technical language because everyone has
a body and everyone knows what it feels like when it stops working properly.

Key structural features: failures have **symptoms** (visible indicators),
**causes** (often hidden), a **sufferer** (the entity experiencing the
failure), and sometimes a **recovery** path. The failure can be gradual
(degradation) or sudden (collapse).
