The domain of threats, vulnerabilities, and failure modes in technical systems.
Encompasses single points of failure, attack surfaces, blast radii, and cascading
breakdowns. Structurally characterized by the tension between a system's intended
function and the ways it can be subverted, degraded, or destroyed -- often through
the very features that make it powerful.
