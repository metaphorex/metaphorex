The deliberate construction of complex systems -- whether software,
mechanical, organizational, or sociotechnical. Encompasses the choices
about decomposition, integration, and the tradeoffs between simplicity
and capability. A frequent target domain for biological and evolutionary
metaphors because designed systems share structural problems with evolved
ones: managing complexity, handling failure, and adapting to changing
environments.
