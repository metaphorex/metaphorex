Games of chance and calculated risk -- poker, dice, roulette, and their
derivatives. The frame's power comes from the interplay between skill and
luck, the irreversibility of wagers, and the asymmetry between house and
player. As a source domain, gambling highlights the role of chance in
outcomes, the cost of participation (the ante), and the distinction between
playing conservatively and going all in.
