Systems and institutions that regulate behavior through coercion,
surveillance, incentive structures, or normative pressure. This frame
encompasses both explicit mechanisms (laws, punishments, hierarchies)
and implicit ones (social norms, algorithmic nudges, architectural
constraints). The central tension is between order and autonomy --
the controller's claim to maintain stability versus the subject's
experience of constraint.
