The human activity of building software: teams, timelines, processes,
and the social dynamics of collaborative programming. Distinct from
software-architecture (which concerns the artifact's structure) and
software-programs (which concerns the running artifact). Software
development is where manufacturing metaphors most often land -- and
most often mislead -- because the activity looks like production but
behaves like design.
