The administration of entities through formal rules, territories, and
transitions of authority. Governance implies defined states (peace,
war, emergency), recognized transitions between them, and bureaucratic
structures that maintain registries and enforce policies. This frame
maps onto software patterns where systems move between well-defined
states according to rules, and where centralized registries track and
control access to resources.
