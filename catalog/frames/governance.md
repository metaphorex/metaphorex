The administration of entities through formal rules, territories, and
bureaucratic structures. Governance implies recognized authorities,
jurisdictions with defined boundaries, and bureaucratic institutions
that maintain registries and enforce policies. This frame maps onto
software patterns where centralized registries track and control
access to resources through official channels.
