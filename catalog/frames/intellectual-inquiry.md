The pursuit of knowledge through theorizing, reasoning, and analysis.
Encompasses theories, ideas, understanding, and intellectual work. As a target
domain, it draws on an extraordinary range of sources -- buildings, food,
vision, plants, people, cutting instruments, fashion -- each highlighting a
different aspect of what it means to think.
