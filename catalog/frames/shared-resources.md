Any pool of finite resources accessed by multiple parties under some
governance regime. Generalizes the commons pattern beyond land to bandwidth,
attention, budgets, and open-source labor. The key structural tension is
between open access and sustainable use, mediated by governance rules that
may be formal or emergent.
