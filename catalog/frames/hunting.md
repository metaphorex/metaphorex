The domain of hunting and trapping -- setting snares, pursuing prey,
catching game, and defending against predators. The frame encodes
anticipation (setting a trap before the event), specificity (traps are
set for particular prey), and the asymmetry between hunter and hunted.
In computing, the hunting vocabulary surfaces in signal handling (trap,
catch), exception handling (throw, catch), and security (honeypots,
phishing). The defensive variant -- trapping something before it causes
harm -- is especially productive.
