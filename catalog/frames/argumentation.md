Structured discourse aimed at establishing or refuting a claim through
evidence and reasoning. Often metaphorically structured as war (attacking
positions, defending claims) but has its own internal logic of claims,
warrants, and rebuttals. The frame highlights how knowledge is contested
and negotiated rather than simply discovered.
