The exchange of meaning between agents through language, gesture, and other
symbolic systems. Communication involves not just the transmission of content
but the collaborative construction of understanding between participants.
As a target domain, it is overwhelmingly structured by the conduit metaphor --
sending, receiving, packaging, and delivering -- which highlights transfer
while obscuring the interpretive and social dimensions of the process.
