The mechanism by which differential reproductive success drives population
change over generations. Structurally compelling because it produces
design-like outcomes without a designer — variation is blind, selection is
not. Frequently borrowed to explain markets, ideas, and technologies, though
the mapping often smuggles in teleology that the original frame lacks.
