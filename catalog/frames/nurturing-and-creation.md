The dynamics of care, sustenance, and generative creation -- and the
dependency structures they produce. Nurturing systems provide the
conditions for growth but also define the boundaries within which growth
can occur. Structurally productive because every nurturing relationship
contains a tension between enabling autonomy and maintaining dependency,
between the generative and the devouring.
