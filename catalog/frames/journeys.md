Travel from origin to destination along a path, encountering obstacles and
making choices at forks. The dominant narrative metaphor in Western culture --
life, love, arguments, and careers are all journeys. Structurally rich because
it provides direction (progress vs. regression), choice points (crossroads),
impediments (roadblocks), and an evaluative endpoint (arrival or failure to
arrive).
