The large-scale cultivation of crops and raising of livestock for food and
material production. Distinguished from horticulture by scale, mechanization,
and an emphasis on storage and distribution infrastructure. Agricultural
frames introduce concepts of yield, surplus, spoilage prevention, seasonal
rhythm, and the built structures (barns, silos, granaries) that manage
harvested material over time.
