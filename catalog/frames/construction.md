The planned assembly of physical structures from raw materials according to
a design. Provides the dominant metaphor family for software development
(building, architecture, foundations) and knowledge work more broadly.
Structurally rich because it separates design from execution, and foregrounds
the idea that invisible foundations determine visible stability.
