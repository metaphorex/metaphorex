Boxing, wrestling, MMA, and other regulated combat disciplines where
physical confrontation is formalized through weight divisions, scoring
systems, and safety protocols. The domain is rich for software metaphors
because it foregrounds physical constraints (weight classes), disciplined
preparation (training camps, weight cuts), and the tension between raw
power and technical skill. Weight divisions are particularly productive:
they create a taxonomy of capability based on a quantifiable physical
constraint, making "lightweight" and "heavyweight" intuitive metaphors
for resource consumption and system demands.
