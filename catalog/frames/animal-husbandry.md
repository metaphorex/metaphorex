The raising, breeding, and management of domesticated animals. Source domain
for a rich family of metaphors about governance, resource management, and
care: the commons (shared grazing land), pets vs cattle (infrastructure
philosophy), herding cats (coordination difficulty), black sheep (deviance),
and shepherding (leadership as tending). Distinct from horticulture in that
the managed entities have agency, move, and resist — which maps well onto
people, processes, and organizations.
