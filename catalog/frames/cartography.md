The art of representing spatial reality in compressed, selective form. Every
map is a deliberate distortion — choosing what to include, what to omit, and
how to project a curved world onto a flat surface. Powerful as a source domain
for any act of modeling or abstraction, because it makes the cost of
representation visible: all maps lie, but some are useful.
