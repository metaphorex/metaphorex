The practice of attracting and catching fish through deliberate use of bait,
lures, and technique. Fishing frames introduce the structural contrast
between the agent who knows the bait is artificial and the target who does
not, the patience required while waiting for a reaction, and the distinction
between active pursuit and passive enticement. Trolling — dragging bait
slowly through water from a moving boat — is a specific fishing technique
that names the original internet behavior of posting provocative content
to elicit reactions.
