Work performed under coercion rather than choice -- slavery, serfdom,
corvee, or any arrangement where the worker has no autonomy over whether,
when, or how to labor. The frame's essential structure is an asymmetric
relationship between a directing intelligence (the master) and an executing
body (the laborer), connected by compulsion rather than contract. As a
source domain, forced labor imports assumptions about obedience,
expendability, and the ever-present possibility of revolt.
