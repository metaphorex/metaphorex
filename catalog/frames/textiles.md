The craft of making cloth from fibers -- spinning, weaving, knitting,
stitching, and dyeing. Textiles predate written language, and their
vocabulary has migrated into abstract domains everywhere: we weave
narratives, spin stories, thread arguments together, and unravel
mysteries. The structural richness comes from the fact that cloth is
made by interlacing many individual elements into a coherent whole,
making it a natural source domain for any composite intellectual product.
