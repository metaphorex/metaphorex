The physics of liquids and gases in motion — flow rates, pressure
differentials, turbulence, and containment. Extremely productive as a source
domain because it provides intuitive models for anything that moves through
conduits: information, money, traffic, emotions. The interplay of pressure,
capacity, and resistance maps onto most throughput problems.
