Preparation, consumption, and digestion of food. Encompasses raw ingredients,
cooking processes, taste, satiation, and nourishment. As a source domain, it
maps intellectual activity onto alimentary processes -- ideas are consumed,
digested, and either nourish or sicken. The embodied grounding is strong:
everyone knows what it feels like to chew on something tough.
