Romantic love, partnerships, and their arc from attraction through commitment
to possible dissolution. One of the richest target domains in any language --
love is understood through journeys, war, madness, physical forces, magic, and
collaborative art. The sheer number of source domains mapped onto love reveals
how resistant the experience is to literal description.
