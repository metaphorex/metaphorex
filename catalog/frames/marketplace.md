The marketplace frame represents open trading environments — bazaars, markets, exchanges — where multiple independent actors transact freely. Key structural features: decentralized control, transparent pricing, competition, and organic self-organization. Unlike managed economies or hierarchical command structures, the marketplace derives its order from the aggregate of countless voluntary interactions rather than from central direction.
