The structured expectations attached to positions within social systems —
parent, teacher, leader, stranger. Roles exist independently of their
occupants, can be performed well or badly, and carry scripts that constrain
behavior. Structurally interesting because the gap between role and occupant
creates space for performance, subversion, and conflict.
