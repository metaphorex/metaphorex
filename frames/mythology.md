Sacred or traditional narratives that encode a culture's understanding of
origins, values, and the boundaries of the possible. Mythological figures
undergo transformations and cross boundaries (mortal/divine, human/animal)
that ordinary beings cannot. Productive as a source domain because myths
compress complex cultural knowledge into memorable archetypal patterns.
