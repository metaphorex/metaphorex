Organized violent conflict between groups, with clear sides, territorial
stakes, and escalating commitment. One of the most pervasive source domains
in everyday language — we attack arguments, defend positions, and marshal
evidence. The frame's deep role structure (allies, enemies, weapons,
territory) maps cleanly onto almost any adversarial situation.
