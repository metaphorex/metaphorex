Structured conversation aimed at evaluating technical options and reaching
a decision. Design reviews, architecture discussions, code reviews, RFCs.

The social dynamics often overshadow the technical content — participants
jockey for status, defend territory, and signal expertise. This makes
technical discussion a fertile target domain for combat, performance, and
dominance metaphors.
