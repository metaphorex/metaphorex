The measurement and optimization of how well a system converts inputs to
outputs under load. Dominated by the concept of the bottleneck — the single
constraint that limits overall throughput. Provides productive metaphors for
any domain where capacity, demand, and constraint interact, from
manufacturing to organizational management.
