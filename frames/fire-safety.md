The practice of containing and preventing the spread of fire through
barriers, resistant materials, and regular inspection. The core structural
insight is compartmentalization — fire doors and walls limit damage by
preventing propagation. Maps cleanly onto any domain where failures must
be isolated to prevent cascading collapse.
